Looking to find and hire great talent?

Nightingale HR Solutions provide a full range of HR services to businesses of all sizes and industries. Our core services include manpower services, resources on a contract basis, employee verification, and HR consultancy. We provide businesses with access to skilled and reliable workers on a temporary or contract basis...

Our Values

Our company is built on the values of trust, respect, integrity, and transparency. We believe that these values are essential in providing the best possible HR service to our clients. We are committed to always acting in the best interests of our clients, and to providing a high level of quality and professionalism in everything we do. We work hard to build relationships and trust with our clients, and we always put their needs first.

Our Vision

Our vision is to provide our clients with optimal HR services that satisfy their unique needs and requirements, enabling them to advance toward growth and success. We want to be the one-stop solution for our clients for any HR-specific needs and a credible resource for job seekers who want to build their careers with the right organisation. We are committed to offering excellent HR services that bring delight and tangible results to our clients.

Our Mission

At Nightingale, our mission is to be the go-to resource for organisations that understand that their people are the key to success. We want to be an accountable resource for businesses and job seekers by providing top-tier Human Resources services tailored to meet each client's unique needs. We're passionate about helping businesses reach their potential through strategic talent recruitment and development, so everyone can benefit from our HR service.

Privacy Policy

Permanent & Contract Staffing
We provide staffing solutions for both permanent and contract positions. We have a wide variety of HR consultancy services that can be tailored to your specific needs. We are here to help you find the right staff for your organisation, whether you are looking for short-term or long-term solutions.
Recruitment Process Outsourcing
A team of experienced HR professionals who can help you with all your RPO needs. With our RPO service, you can focus on your core business while we take care of the recruitment process for you.
Talent Deployment & Outsourcing
We provide comprehensive consultancy services to help you source and deploy the best talent for your organisation. We have a team of experienced and qualified consultants who have a deep understanding to find you the right talent.
Employment Verification
By verifying the employment history of new and current employees, you can help protect your business from possible lawsuits and other legal problems. In addition, by checking references and conducting background checks, you can help ensure that your employees are honest and trustworthy.
Training & Development
As an HR consultancy, we offer training and development services to help your employees enhance their skills and performance. We can provide customised training programs that are tailored to your company's needs and objectives.
Core HR Services
We provide a comprehensive HR consultancy service that covers all aspects of HR, from recruitment and selection to employee relations and payroll. We provide a complete end-to-end service that is tailored to the specific needs of each client.
